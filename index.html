Eligibility Criteria for the Program

To be eligible to join the Tri-Shakti Officers Training Institute, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • Age Requirement: All applicants must be over 18 years of age. This ensures that participants are mature enough to handle the rigors of the training and are legally adults.
  • Educational Qualification: Applicants must have successfully completed their +2 level education or its equivalent. This academic requirement guarantees that all candidates have a fundamental level of education that is necessary for the understanding and absorption of the training material.
  • Nationality: Candidates must be Nepali citizens. This requirement is in place to prioritize the training for nationals, aligning with the objectives of strengthening local security forces.
  • Language Proficiency: A basic knowledge of English is required. The program includes materials and instructions that are partially in English. Therefore, a basic understanding of the language is necessary for effective communication and comprehension during the course.

These criteria are designed to ensure that all candidates entering the program are well-prepared and have a solid foundation to build upon. Our training program is rigorous and requires dedication, commitment, and a willingness to learn. If you meet these criteria and are looking for an opportunity to grow and develop your potential, we encourage you to apply.

How Can I Apply?

The application process for the Tri-Shakti Officers Training Institute is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Contact Us: Reach out to us via a phone call. Our team is ready to guide you through the process and provide all the necessary information you need.
  2. Register for a Seat: Once you've received all the details, you can register for an available seat in our upcoming training batch. Registration is important to secure your spot in the program.
  3. Participation Confirmation: After registering, you will receive a confirmation call to participate in the training. This step ensures your commitment and readiness for the program.
  4. Selection Process: Post-training, candidates are sent for the selection process, where their suitability for security forces will be assessed based on the training performance and other criteria.

We are dedicated to making the application process as smooth and transparent as possible. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us.

What is the Selection Process?

The selection process at the Tri-Shakti Officers Training Institute is designed to assess the suitability of candidates for roles in security forces. It involves several stages:

  1. Initial Screening: Applicants are first screened based on their application forms, eligibility criteria, and other preliminary requirements.
  2. Written Examination: Candidates undergo a written examination that tests their knowledge, intelligence, and problem-solving skills.
  3. Physical Fitness Test: Physical endurance and fitness are crucial. Candidates must pass a series of physical tests.
  4. Psychological Assessment: This stage assesses the mental robustness and psychological suitability of the candidates for the demanding nature of security roles.
  5. Personal Interview: In the final stage, candidates face an interview panel where their motivation, communication skills, and overall personality are evaluated.

The selection process is rigorous and thorough, ensuring that only the most suitable candidates are chosen for the prestigious responsibility of serving in the security forces.

How Can I Prepare?

Preparing for the Tri-Shakti Officers Training Institute involves several key steps to ensure you are fully ready for both the training program and the selection process:

  • Understand the Requirements: Familiarize yourself with the eligibility criteria, selection process, and the physical and mental demands of the training.
  • Enhance Physical Fitness: Start a regular fitness regime focusing on strength, endurance, and agility. Physical fitness is a critical component of the training.
  • Brush Up on Essential Skills: Work on necessary skills like communication, teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving, which are vital for success in the program.
  • Study Relevant Material: Prepare for the written examination by studying relevant materials, including general knowledge, current affairs, and any specialized subjects pertinent to the training.
  • Mental Preparation: Build mental resilience through practices like meditation, stress management techniques, and positive visualization to prepare for the psychological aspects of the training and selection process.

Preparation is key to success in our program. Dedicate time to physical and mental training, and you’ll be well on your way to achieving your goals with Tri-Shakti.
